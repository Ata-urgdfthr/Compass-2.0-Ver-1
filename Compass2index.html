Share settings

The differentiator. Like a Google Sheet: invite by named email, pick per-section visibility (or all), revoke per-person anytime. To change an existing share, tap the person under "Shared with" — their section toggles reappear pre-set to what they currently see; flip any and Save. The change is instant and the viewer's screen updates live — no message needed.
